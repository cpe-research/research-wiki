# Open Research Questions

Questions that need evidence. When resolved, move to [resolved.md](resolved.md) with the answer and sources.

## AVGO

### Customer Concentration
- **What is AVGO's real customer concentration risk?** AI revenue ($8.4B Q1 FY2026) comes from ~6 hyperscaler XPU customers. If one pauses or delays, what is the revenue impact? Need: customer-level revenue breakdowns (likely unavailable in filings, may require expert calls).

### Supply Chain
- **How does TSMC capacity constrain AVGO's AI revenue ramp?** TSMC supplies ~95% of wafers. Advanced packaging (CoWoS) is the primary bottleneck in 2026. What is the capacity allocation timeline and how does it affect the $100B 2027 target?

### Competitive
- **Will hyperscalers develop in-house ASIC design capability?** Google has TPU. Will Meta, Microsoft, or others build their own custom silicon teams instead of using Broadcom? What signals would indicate this shift?
- **What is Marvell's realistic competitive threat in custom AI ASICs?** MRVL full-year FY2026 revenue ($8.2B total) is less than one quarter of AVGO's AI revenue. But are they winning new XPU customers?

### Financial
- **What is AVGO's organic growth rate ex-VMware?** FY2024 revenue jump was largely VMware consolidation. Need to separate organic semiconductor growth from M&A.
